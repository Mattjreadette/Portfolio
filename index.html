About

My name is Matt Readette I am a PhD candidate in the the Faculty of Creative Industries, Education and Social Justice (School of Teacher Education and Leadership) at QUT. My research project explore the post study abroad experiences of alumni and how the acquired skills and competencies upon return from overseas are realised and expressed in their professional fields. I am interestd in how this relates to international relations and higher education soft power initiatives.
